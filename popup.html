SMO Worklog Tool

Easily log your work hours to SRA SmartOSC

Quick Start:

  1. Click "Start Logging Work" button below
  2. Your access token will be automatically captured from your browsing session, or you can paste it manually
  3. Choose your method:
    • Manual Input: Select your own date range and project
    • Auto: Automatically generate worklog from start of month to today based on allocated data
  4. Review, modify if needed, and submit your worklog!
